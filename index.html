Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open multiple URLs at once?

Paste your list of URLs into the text area above (one URL per line) and click Open URLs. Each link opens in a new browser tab automatically.

Is the Bulk URL Opener free?

Yes — 100% free, no account, no registration, and nothing to install. It runs entirely in your browser.

How many URLs can I open at once?

There is no hard limit. URLs open sequentially with a short delay to avoid being blocked by your browser's pop-up blocker.

Why is my browser blocking the new tabs?

Some browsers block rapid tab openings. If tabs are being blocked, allow pop-ups for this site in your browser settings, then try again.

Does it work offline?

The tool itself runs offline — no server request is made. You still need an internet connection to load the URLs you open.
